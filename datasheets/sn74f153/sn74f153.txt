These data selectors/multiplexers contain
inverters and drivers to supply full binary decoding
data selection to the AND-OR gates. Separate
strobe (G) inputs are provided for each of the two
4-line sections.
The SN54F153 is characterized for operation over
the full military temperature range of –55°C to
125°C. The SN74F153 is characterized for
operation from 0°C to 70°C.
