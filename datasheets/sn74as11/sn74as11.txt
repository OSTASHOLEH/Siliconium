These devices contain three independent 3-input
positive-AND gates. They perform the Boolean
functions Y = A • B • C or Y = A + B + C in positive
logic.
The SN54ALS11A and SN54AS11 are
characterized for operation over the full military
temperature range of –55°C to 125°C. The
SN74ALS11A and SN74AS11 are characterized
for operation from 0°C to 70°C.
